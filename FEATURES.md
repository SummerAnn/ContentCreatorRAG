## Analysis & Research Tools

### Viral Video Analyzer
- **Single Video Analysis**: Analyze any video from YouTube, TikTok, or Instagram
  - Download and transcribe videos automatically
  - Extract hook (first 3 seconds)
  - Analyze story structure (setup, conflict, resolution, CTA)
  - Identify key moments and why they work
  - Get remix suggestions for your niche
  - Visual style analysis
  
- **Batch Channel Analysis**: Analyze entire channels
  - Get list of all videos with metadata
  - Sort by views to find top performers
  - Identify patterns in successful content

### Content Sorter
- Sort Instagram or TikTok content by:
  - Views
  - Likes
  - Comments
  - Engagement Rate
  - Date
  
- Export data to:
  - CSV
  - JSON
  - Excel

### Transcription Service
- **File Upload**: Transcribe uploaded audio/video files
- **URL Transcription**: Transcribe videos directly from YouTube, TikTok, etc.
- **Caption Generation**: Automatically generate SRT or VTT caption files
- **Language Detection**: Automatic language detection
- **Word Count & Duration**: Detailed transcription statistics
